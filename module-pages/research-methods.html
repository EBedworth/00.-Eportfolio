Reaserch Methods and Professional Practice

Research Methods and Professional Practice was the penultimate module of my MSc Computer Science course with the University of Essex. I began this module in July 2024.

Click on the link in the 'Description & Link' column to navigate to the respective piece of work.

Unit Work

Name Unit(s) Description & Link
Collaborative Discussion 1 1, 2, 3 Review the application of the ACM Code of Ethics to a case study from the examples on the ACM website.
Reflective Activity 1 - Ethics in Computing 1 Review an article about global ethical opinions of AI developments and consider next steps to be taken.
Literature Review Outline 2 Present an outline of the literature review which will be undertaken during Assignment 1.
Case Study on Privacy 4 Consider some ethical situations from a case study on a situation where a researcher requests personally-identifiable information.
Reflective Activity 2 - Case Study on the Inapproriate Use of Surveys 5 Review the Cambridge Analytica scandal and provide reflection of how it happened, alongside other examples of inappropriate use of survey data.
Wiki Activity - Questionnaires 5 Finding and critically analysing a research questionnaire.
Collaborative Discussion 2 7, 8, 9 Review ethical considerations for a case study about a researcher handling non-favourable research results.
Statistics Worksheets 7, 8, 9 Statstic worksheets in excel, covering research statistic calculations.
